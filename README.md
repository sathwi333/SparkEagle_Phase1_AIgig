## 📌 Problem Statement
Gig delivery workers in India rely on daily earnings to support their families. However, external disruptions such as heavy rain, extreme heat, high pollution levels, and sudden curfews reduce their ability to work. These conditions can lead to a loss of 20–30% of their weekly income. Currently, there is no system that protects gig workers from such income loss, forcing them to bear the financial burden alone.

---

## 👤 Target Persona
Rajesh Kumar is a 30-year-old delivery partner working for platforms like Zomato and Swiggy in a metro city. He earns ₹4,000–₹6,000 per week depending on the number of deliveries. During monsoons or extreme heat, his work hours reduce significantly, affecting his income. As the sole breadwinner of his family, any income loss directly impacts his household expenses.

---

## 💡 Solution
We propose an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed specifically for gig workers. The system automatically detects external disruptions using real-time data and triggers instant payouts without requiring manual claims. This ensures that workers receive compensation quickly and efficiently when their ability to work is affected.

---

## 🔄 Workflow
1. **User Onboarding:** Users register through a mobile app and provide basic details.  
2. **Risk Profiling:** AI analyzes user location, work patterns, and environmental risks.  
3. **Policy Creation:** Users select a weekly insurance plan based on risk level.  
4. **Real-Time Monitoring:** The system continuously tracks weather and environmental data.  
5. **Claim Triggering:** When predefined thresholds are met, claims are triggered automatically.  
6. **Instant Payout:** Compensation is directly transferred to the user’s account.

---

## 💰 Weekly Premium Model
The platform uses a flexible weekly pricing model to match gig workers’ earning cycles:
- **Low Risk:** ₹50–₹75/week with basic coverage  
- **Medium Risk:** ₹100–₹150/week with moderate coverage  
- **High Risk:** ₹150–₹200/week with maximum protection  

Premiums are dynamically adjusted using AI based on location risk and user behavior.

---

## ⚡ Parametric Triggers
Payouts are triggered automatically when objective thresholds are met:
- 🌧 Rainfall exceeding 50mm in 24 hours  
- 🌡 Temperature above 40°C  
- 🌫 Air Quality Index (AQI) above 300  
- 🚫 Government-declared curfews or strikes  

These triggers ensure transparency and eliminate manual claim processes.

---

## 🤖 AI/ML Engine
The system integrates AI/ML for intelligent decision-making:
- **Risk Prediction:** Forecast disruption probability using historical and real-time data  
- **Dynamic Pricing:** Adjust premiums based on risk levels  
- **Fraud Detection:** Identify suspicious patterns in claims  
- **Income Prediction:** Estimate expected earnings and potential loss  

---

## 🛠 Platform & Technology
- **Frontend:** React Native (Mobile App)  
- **Backend:** Node.js with Express  
- **Database:** MYSQL 
- **AI/ML:** Python (TensorFlow, Scikit-learn)  
- **APIs:** Weather API, AQI API, Payment Gateway (UPI)  

---

## 🛡 Adversarial Defense & Anti-Spoofing Strategy

### 🔍 Differentiation
The system differentiates genuine users from fraudsters using behavioral analysis:
- Detects unnatural movement patterns (teleporting, unrealistic speeds)  
- Matches delivery activity with location data  
- Analyzes historical behavior patterns  

### 📊 Data Beyond GPS
To prevent spoofing, multiple data points are used:
- Device ID and device fingerprinting  
- IP address tracking and VPN detection  
- Sensor data (accelerometer, motion, speed)  
- Delivery activity logs (orders completed)  
- Historical claim patterns  

### ⚖️ UX Balance
The system ensures fairness for genuine users:
- **Low Risk Users:** Instant payouts  
- **Medium Risk Users:** Soft verification (OTP/live location)  
- **High Risk Users:** Claims flagged for review  
- Grace handling for network issues during bad weather  

---

## 📊 Impact
This solution provides financial security to gig workers by ensuring stable income during disruptions. It reduces financial stress, improves worker trust, and creates a scalable insurance model for the growing gig economy.
